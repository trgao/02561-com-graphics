Projection shadows:
Pros:
- Much easier to implement compared to shadow mapping
- Much faster and less resources needed to compute
Cons:
- Can only work to project shadows on flat surfaces
- Cannot project shadows onto objects themselves

Shadow mapping:
Pros:
- Can create shadows of greater complexities compared to projection shadows
- Can cast self shadows
Cons:
- Much more complicated to implement
- Takes more resources to compute shadows
- Shadow aliasing/pixelation
